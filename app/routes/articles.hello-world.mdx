## How it all started

My fascination with data and AI began during my undergraduate studies in Computer Science at LP University, where I specialized in Machine Learning. What started as academic curiosity quickly turned into a passion when I published my first research paper on Driver Drowsiness Detection Using AI. Seeing how machine learning could solve real-world safety problems was the spark that ignited my career in data science.

## Building at Scale — Inviz.ai

My first major industry role was at Inviz.ai, where I worked as a Data Scientist for over two years. This is where I truly learned what it means to build ML systems at scale. Working with clients like Nasdaq, Croma, and Tatacliq, I was responsible for:

- **User Personalization**: Designing behavioral segmentation models that analyzed 20M+ user interactions, boosting content recommendation CTR by 37%
- **Search Ranking**: Engineering event-level clickstream pipelines on VertexAI using PySpark and Airflow, processing 6M+ daily signals
- **Causal Experimentation**: Running A/B experiments with difference-in-differences methodology, validating an 18% CTR lift across 20M+ users

The most rewarding part was seeing how data-driven decisions could directly impact business outcomes — our uplift modeling work alone drove $4.2M in incremental revenue recovery.

## Graduate School at Michigan State University

In 2024, I made the decision to pursue my Master's in Data Science at Michigan State University, and it's been an incredible journey. With a 4.0 GPA, I've been diving deep into advanced topics while also contributing to meaningful research at the Julian Samora Research Institute.

My research work involves fine-tuning T5 transformer models for sentiment and policy signal modeling on large-scale document corpora, helping inform Latino community policy strategy. It's a perfect intersection of NLP, social good, and rigorous statistical methodology.

## Industry Experience During Grad School

Even during my graduate studies, I've continued gaining industry experience:

- At **BorgWarner**, I fine-tuned LLM classification pipelines using LangChain and Databricks, improving decision-routing accuracy by 36%
- At **Kings Crowd**, I engineered classification pipelines for startup risk valuation, achieving 86.7% AUC across 10K+ startups

## What's Next

I'm particularly excited about the intersection of Generative AI and practical business applications. My recent project, [Nexora](https://nexora-api-73508499658.us-central1.run.app/), is a RAG-powered e-commerce platform that demonstrates how LLMs, knowledge graphs, and vector search can transform product discovery. I believe we're just scratching the surface of what's possible when we combine traditional ML with modern GenAI capabilities.

I'm always looking for opportunities to push the boundaries of AI — whether that's through research, building production systems, or collaborating with other passionate engineers and scientists.
